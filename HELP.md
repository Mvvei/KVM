# Kindle Vocabulary Mate - User Guide

## What is Kindle Vocabulary Mate?

Kindle Vocabulary Mate helps you build your vocabulary while reading. Capture words from Kindle (or any reading app), look up definitions, and organize them by book source for easy review and learning.

---

## Getting Started

### Adding Words to Your Vocabulary

1. **While reading in Kindle (or any app):**
   - Select and copy text that contains a word
   - Tap the **Share** button
   - Choose **"Kindle Mate"** from the share menu

2. **The Share Screen will appear showing:**
   - **Word** - The extracted word from your selection
   - **Source** - The book title and author
   - **Definition** - Tap "Look Up" to fetch the definition

3. **Review the Definition:**
   - Multiple definitions will appear with checkboxes
   - Select the definition(s) you want to keep
   - You can edit the word or definition text if needed

4. **Save the Word:**
   - Tap the **"Save"** button at the bottom
   - A confirmation message will appear
   - The word is now saved to your vocabulary list

## Using the Main App

### Viewing Your Vocabulary

- Open the **Kindle Mate** app
- Words are automatically **grouped by book source** (book title and author)
- Each word shows:
  - **Word text** in bold
  - **Definition preview** (first 80 characters)
  - **Date saved**
  - **Book icon** (filled = has definition, outline = no definition)

### Viewing Word Details

1. **Tap any word** to view full details
2. **In the detail view you'll see:**
   - Full word text
   - Complete definition (if available)
   - Source book information
   - Date/time saved

### Navigating Between Words

**Swipe gestures in detail view:**
- **Swipe UP** ‚Üí Go to next word

### Deleting Words

### Best Practices for Capturing Words

**Expected text format from Kindle:**
```
"word"

‚Äî Book Title by Author Name
https://a.co/booklink
```

The app automatically extracts:
- The word from the quotes
- The book title and author
- Removes the URL

### Managing Your Vocabulary

- **Word Count** - Displayed at the top: "*X words*"
- **Organized by Source** - Words grouped by which book they came from
- **Quick Lookup** - Tap any word to see full definition
- **Easy Deletion** - Swipe to remove unwanted words

### Definitions

- Definitions are fetched from a free dictionary API
- Multiple definitions may appear (different meanings, parts of speech)
- You can select which definitions to keep using checkboxes
- Edit definitions before saving if needed

## Troubleshooting

### "No shared texts yet" appears

- You haven't added any words yet
- Share a word from Kindle to get started

### Definition not loading

- Tap the **"Look Up"** button to retry
- Check your internet connection
- Some words may not be in the dictionary

### Word extraction issues

- Make sure to copy text in this format: `"word" ‚Äî Book by Author`
- The word should be in double quotes
- Include the book title and author line

### App not appearing in Share menu

- Make sure the share extension is enabled
- Try closing and reopening Kindle
- Restart your device if needed
