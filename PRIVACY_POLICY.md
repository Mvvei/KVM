# Privacy Policy for Kindle Vocabulary Mate

**Last updated:** December 29, 2024

## Overview

Kindle Vocabulary Mate ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect any personal information.**

The App does not:
- Collect your name, email, or contact information
- Track your usage or behavior
- Use analytics services
- Display advertisements
- Require user accounts or login

## Data Storage

All vocabulary data you save in the App is stored **locally on your device only**.

- Your word lists are stored in your device's local storage
- No data is transmitted to external servers (except dictionary lookups)
- No data is shared with third parties
- No cloud backup or sync (in this version)

## Dictionary API

When you look up a word definition:
- Only the word text is sent to a free dictionary API
- No personal information is included
- The API response (definition) is stored locally on your device
- Dictionary API: https://dictionaryapi.dev/ (Free Dictionary API)

## Permissions

The App requires:

**Share Extension Permission:**
- To receive text shared from other apps (like Kindle)
- You control what you share
- The App only receives what you explicitly share

The App does NOT require:
- Location access
- Camera access
- Contacts access
- Photos access
- Microphone access

## Third-Party Services

This App does not integrate with:
- Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- Advertising networks
- Social media platforms
- Cloud storage services (in this version)

## Children's Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The App is suitable for all ages (rated 4+).

## Data Security

- All data is stored securely on your device using iOS secure storage
- Data is protected by your device's passcode/Face ID/Touch ID
- No data transmission over the internet (except dictionary lookups)

## Your Data Rights

You have complete control over your data:

**Access:** All your data is visible in the App  
**Delete:** Delete individual words by swiping left, or delete the App to remove all data  
**Export:** Export your vocabulary to CSV format  
**Control:** You decide what words to save and when to delete them

## Data Retention

- Data is retained on your device until you delete it
- Deleting the App removes all data permanently
- No data backups are created outside your device

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be:
- Posted in app updates
- Reflected in the "Last updated" date above
- Announced in the App Store release notes

Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
